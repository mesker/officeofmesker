About Me

I’m Michael Mesker, a designer in Chicago. With a background in publication design, I’ve grown to specialize in creating systems for screens of all sizes. Over the last few years, I’ve worked closely with engineers and client teams on large scale redesigns for places like Google Fiber, University of Michigan, The Ounce of Prevention, Trunk Club, and YouTube.

Colophon

This site is built with Jekyll and hosted on Github Pages. On the front-end, I make good use of the Modular Scale and Susy grid gems. While I consider the site a work in progress, the current design decisions are documented in a brief style guide.
